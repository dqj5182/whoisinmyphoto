A photo organizer based on Face Recognition system to ease your burden

This webpage allows you to make several distinct folders that contains photos with specific person. For example, your name is Daniel and you have friends named Michael and John. You want to make three folders named "Michael", "John", and "Daniel" to organize the photos you have with respect to who is in your photo. In the Michael folder, there are photos that has Michael in it. In the John folder, there are all the photos that has John in it. As you may have guessed or wanted, each folder may have overlapping photos with other folder as one photo can have both Michael and John (especially when it is a family photo). Now, if you are fully understood about this webpage, please upload a folder that contains the photos you want to organize at the next page.

Start with making folders of your friends and family!
