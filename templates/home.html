About our webpage

This webpage allows you to make several separate folders that contains specific person.

For example, you have friends named Michael, John and your name is Daniel.

You want to make three folders named "Michael", "John", and "Daniel".

In the Michael folder, there are all the photos that has Michael in it.

In the John folder, there are all the photos that has John in it.

As you may have guessed or wanted, each folder may have overlapping photos as one photo can have both Michael and John.

Now, if you are fully understood about this webpage, please upload a folder that contains the photos you want to process.
